Black Lives Matter

It wasn't until the death of George Floyd {May 25th, 2020} when I finally realized that Black Lives Matter and must be protected, understood, and known about. As an Asian American, I have learned that I was, and am, unknowingly complicit with upholding white supremacy for 22 years.

However, with this newfound knowledge, I have compiled a library for myself and others to begin and continue our journey on unlearning ingrained racism to become better allies.

Important Note

Just because you signed a petition, called a mayor, or learned a new term, it doesn't mean your job is done. You must continually push yourself to learn.

Racism isn't over--not until we can understand where it stems from, actively unlearn it, and radically change our policies that invest more into Black communities.

Hearing, Reading, and Buying from Black People

In order to learn about Black people, we need to learn directly from them. Check out podcasts, books, movies, and find Black owned businesses within Seattle and the Greater Seattle area.
